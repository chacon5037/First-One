About This Website

Customization Site

This website is about designing your Jeans, Shoes or Hoodies to Fit!

Me

My name is Edison Chacon. I was born in Salem, MA, and I live in Lynn where I attend Lynn Classical High School. One of my favorite things to do is play soccer. In the future, I want to add a feature on this site where you can design your own custom jersey with your name and a logo you choose.

Why I Made This

I made this website for people who want to change their outfits to match their own style. I want everyone to enjoy being creative with what they wear and feel confident standing out. Many people buy the same clothes, but style is personal, and I want this site to help people express who they are.

Future Plans

My plan for the future is to expand the website beyond just shoes, sweaters, and hoodies. I want to add more options so users can create full custom outfits that are unique to them. The goal is to give everyone more choices and more ways to design what fits their personality.
